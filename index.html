What Is Learner Experience Design?

Learner Experience Design (LXD) combines the creativity of design with the field of training and development, with aspects of game design, cognitive psychology, and education. In practical terms, that means creating digital experiences that are based on how people learn, how they retain knowledge, and how they apply that knowledge in real-world scenarios.

How Does LXD Fit Your Life?

Life doesn’t stop just because you’re in school. Adding school on top of work, family, and personal commitments can be a big ask.

Program Format

The LXD program is offered entirely online and asynchronously. That means that you can do coursework when it makes sense for you. It doesn’t matter if you do before the sun comes up, after it goes down, or anywhere in between. It also means that you’re not spending time driving to campus; your campus is anywhere you have an internet connection.

Program Length

The certificate is made up of 5 courses over 16 credit hours. Many finish the program in 2-3 semesters (12-18 months). Your academic advisor can help you find the timetable that works best for you and your life.
